About

Yeonjung Hong is a jewelry designer based in New York City. Originally from Korea, she graduated from Rhode Island School of Design with a degree in Jewelry and Metalsmithing. Her interest lies on creating adornments around the body conveying memories of mundane sceneries that are often neglected.
